1. This instance is formed with the top 7 days (in number of services) for each one of the cities.
2. The top 7 days for each city (which might be different from city to city) are randomly assigned to one day of the week of 2025-09-08 to 2025-09-13. All the services of one day go to one and one day alone of the simulated week.
3. The seven days are taken from services of 2025.
4. All the services which have at least one labor of type different to VEHICLE_TRANSPORTATION and missing address_point (which indicates they're not on the address.csv file) are filtered out.
